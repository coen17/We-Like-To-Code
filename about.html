About We Like To Code

We Like To Code is an education site but for everyone we do not aim our courses at just young students we aim them at everyone! We try to push our students skills to the limits! Our team is driven to give you the best start in your coding career! Every week we update our website with code, information and facts about computing and software these fun filled facts we help you in your coding career!

Our Goal

Our goal is to get everyone coding, making their own websites, apps and more we believe that everyone should learn to code and it is our goal to teach you how to. We don't want to use books or a classroom we use what everyone else is using now to get information and you can do just that here at We Like To Code you'll find information about making and uploading your website, making your first app and even making a game so why not come and join us!
